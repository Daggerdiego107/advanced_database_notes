## Topics covered
- UNION and UNION ALL
- INTERSECT and EXCEPT

## What I understood
- `UNION` combines the results of two queries and removes duplicates, while `UNION ALL` combines the results without removing duplicates.
- `INTERSECT` returns only the rows that are present in both queries, while `EXCEPT` returns the rows that are present in the first query but not in the second query.
